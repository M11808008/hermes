ChatGPT 照片轉手作紙雕分層插畫｜Paper-Cut Layered Illustration Prompt 範例

來源為 Threads 帳號 jack41068 的貼文，分享一段可把照片轉成柔軟手作紙雕／剪紙分層插畫的 ChatGPT 影像 prompt。這類風格近期在 Threads 上流行，重點是把人物照片轉譯成 Q 版、粉彩、紙片堆疊、帶白色貼紙外框的立體插畫。

一句話摘要

這段 prompt 的價值在於非常明確地定義了「紙雕分層」的視覺語法：圓潤可愛比例、簡化五官、白色外輪廓、紙層陰影、雷射切割卡紙邊緣、霧面厚紙材質與故事書般的溫柔氛圍。

原始 Prompt

```text

Convert this image into a soft, handcrafted paper-cut layered illustration style, inspired by papercraft diorama aesthetics. Use smooth rounded shapes, simplified cute character proportions, and minimal facial details (dot eyes, blush cheeks) to create a warm, charming look. Apply stacked paper layers with visible depth, subtle shadows between layers, and clean cut edges that resemble laser-cut cardstock. Add a distinct white outer outline layer surrounding each main character, resembling a thick sticker border or white cut-paper backing, clearly separating the characters from the background. This white layer should feel like an intentional paper layer, not a glow. Use a pastel color palette with muted blues, greens, and warm neutrals, balanced and calming. Lighting should feel soft, diffused, and even, enhancing the dimensional paper layers without harsh contrast. Textures should appear matte and tactile, like thick art paper or craft foam. Overall mood: cozy, wholesome, gentle, and storybook-like, with a playful yet polished handcrafted feel suitable for modern illustration, children’s books, or decorative art.

```

中文翻譯版 Prompt

```text

請將這張圖片轉換成柔軟、手工製作的剪紙分層插畫風格，靈感來自紙藝立體模型美學。

使用光滑圓潤的形狀、簡化可愛的人物比例，以及最少量的臉部細節，例如點點眼與腮紅臉頰，創造溫暖迷人的外觀。

請使用堆疊紙層，呈現可見深度、層與層之間的細緻陰影，以及像雷射切割卡紙般乾淨的切邊。

在每個主要角色周圍加入明確的白色外輪廓層，像厚貼紙邊框或白色剪紙背板，讓角色與背景清楚分離。

這個白色層應該像刻意設計的紙層，而不是發光效果。

使用粉彩色調，包含柔和藍色、綠色與溫暖中性色，整體要平衡、安定。

光線應柔和、擴散且均勻，能加強紙層的立體感，但不要有強烈對比。

材質應呈現霧面與可觸摸的厚藝術紙或工藝泡棉質感。

整體氛圍要舒適、健康、溫柔、像故事書一樣，帶有俏皮但精緻的手作感，適合現代插畫、兒童讀物或裝飾藝術。

```

視覺特徵

根據貼文畫面觀察，產出圖像具有以下特徵：

  • 整體像紙材一層層堆疊出的 papercraft diorama，而不是照片濾鏡。
  • 人物比例 Q 版化但不過度誇張，五官簡化為點眼、淡腮紅、柔和表情。
  • 色彩偏低飽和粉彩，常見天空藍、米白、淡粉、淺綠與暖中性色。
  • 主要人物外側有明顯白色描邊，像貼紙邊框或剪紙背板。
  • 背景、人物、花草、雲朵與建築元素有分層陰影，呈現淺浮雕立體感。
  • 邊緣圓潤乾淨，像雷射切割卡紙；材質偏霧面厚紙或工藝泡棉。
  • 氛圍溫柔、療癒、日系／韓系故事書感，適合生活照與情侶照。

欒帥可直接使用的優化版 Prompt

```text

Convert this image into a soft handcrafted paper-cut layered illustration inspired by papercraft diorama aesthetics.

Keep the original subjects, pose, relationship, clothing silhouette, and key background elements recognizable, but simplify everything into smooth rounded cut-paper shapes.

Use cute character proportions with minimal facial details: dot eyes, tiny nose, soft smile, and blush cheeks.

Build the scene from stacked paper layers with visible depth, subtle shadows, clean laser-cut cardstock edges, and matte tactile paper texture.

Add a distinct white outer cut-paper backing around each main character, like a thick sticker border. It must look like a real paper layer, not a glow or light effect.

Use a calm pastel palette with muted blues, greens, creams, warm neutrals, and gentle accent colors.

Lighting should be soft, diffused, and even, enhancing the paper depth without harsh contrast.

Overall mood: cozy, wholesome, gentle, storybook-like, playful but polished, suitable for modern illustration, children’s books, greeting cards, or decorative art.

Avoid realistic skin texture, glossy 3D rendering, hard shadows, hyperrealism, and overly detailed facial features.

```

使用注意事項

  • 若人物很多，需加上 `keep all people visible and recognizable`，避免漏人。
  • 若要保留地點特色，加入 `preserve key background landmarks`。
  • 若白色外框變成光暈，強調 `not a glow, not a halo, it must be a physical cut-paper backing`。
  • 若效果太像 3D 玩具，加入 `avoid glossy 3D toy texture`。
  • 若臉部太寫實或怪異，加入 `minimal facial details only, no realistic facial rendering`。
